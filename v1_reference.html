The Model

One engine.
Infinite brand skins.

We're retail-first by choice — not by constraint. We believe the fastest path to velocity is building brands your customers already follow. Our founders built social-first brands to 2M+ followers and 2B+ views. We use that reach to drive sell-through for our retail partners, not compete with them.

01 — Product Engine
Core SKUs, perfected.
Every Hidden Supply product starts as a proven format — wax candy, gummies, novelty candy. We manufacture at scale with quality and margin built in from day one. High-velocity SKUs that turn fast and fill carts.
02 — Brand Skins
Tailored per retailer.
Each core product wears its own brand identity. Same great product, different story — letting us match every retailer's customer culture without redesigning from scratch. Your shelf, your customer, your brand.
03 — Social Pull
We drive the demand.
We don't just ship and hope. Our founders built brands to 2M+ followers and 2B+ views. That playbook drives customers into your store, amplifies sell-through, and makes your shelf the one they come back to.
