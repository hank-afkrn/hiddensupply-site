How It Works

Three moves.
One discovery.

Candy that actually does something. That's the whole idea.

01 Open.

Pull back the packaging. Inside: something that looks a little like a science kit and a lot like dessert. Curiosity engaged.

02 Experiment.

Follow the steps — or don't. Mix. Grow. Play. The format is half the fun. This is candy with instructions, and that's a feature, not a quirk.
