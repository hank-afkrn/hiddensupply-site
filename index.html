The Model

One engine.
Infinite brand skins.

We're retail-first by design. Core products manufactured at scale, wrapped in brand identities tailored per retailer. The result: high-velocity SKUs that turn fast, look great on shelf, and carry a social audience that drives customers straight to the store.

01 — Product Engine
Core SKUs, built to turn.
Every Hidden Supply product starts as a proven format — wax candy, gummies, novelty candy. Manufactured at scale with quality and margin locked in from day one. High-velocity SKUs your customers reach for on repeat.
02 — Brand Skins
Your shelf. Your customer.
Each core product wears its own brand identity. Same great product, different story — letting us match every retailer's customer without redesigning from scratch. Specialty, off-price, or impulse — we speak the language.
03 — Social Pull
We drive demand in.
We don't just ship and hope. Our founders built brands to 2M+ followers and 2B+ views. That playbook runs for every Hidden Supply brand — driving customers into your store before they even know they're looking for candy.
